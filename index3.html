    Bicep Curl

    The bicep curl is a fundamental exercise for building bicep strength. Hold a dumbbell in each hand with palms facing forward.

    Technique

    Keep your upper arms stationary and curl the weights while exhaling. Lift the dumbbells until your biceps are fully contracted. Slowly lower them back to the starting position.

    Benefits:

    This exercise effectively isolates the biceps, promoting muscle growth and improving arm strength.

    Wrist Curl

    The wrist curl is an essential exercise for strengthening the forearm muscles. Hold a dumbbell in each hand with palms facing upward.

    Technique

    Rest your forearms on a bench or your thighs, allowing your wrists to hang over the edge. Curl the weights up by flexing your wrists, then slowly lower them back to the starting position.

    Benefits:

    This exercise isolates the forearm flexors, improving grip strength and enhancing overall wrist stability.
